Here are some tips for securing your password from hackers


  • -Make sure to use capital letters(use lower case too)!
  • -Any Symbols Vastly Improve The Strength of the Password, this may include # or even numbers!
  • -Make your password over 8 characters long
  • -Have a wide variety of passwords, don't use an important password for untrustworthy sites!
  • -Be careful where you use any of your passwords, we're becoming more and more vulnerable to websites being hijacked and people's information being stolen!
